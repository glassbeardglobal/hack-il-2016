Inception

2010

Dom Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio) is a thief with the rare ability
to enter people's dreams and steal their secrets from
their subconscious. His skill has made him a hot commodity in the
world of corporate espionage but has also cost him
everything he loves. Cobb gets a chance at redemption when he
is offered a seemingly impossible task: Plant an idea in
someone's mind. If he succeeds, it will be the perfect crime,
but a dangerous enemy anticipates Cobb's every move.
